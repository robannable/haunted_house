<em>/home</em>

This project uses a Large Language Model (LLM) to simulate a conversation with a house. Responses are a combination of information present in the model's dataset plus chosen documents related to their work and associated texts, using Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) to fine tune the knowledge base.

Coded with the assistance of <a href="https://www.perplexity.ai/">Perplexity AI and API service</a>.
